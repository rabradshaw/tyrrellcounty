Sheriff’s Office Permit:

Office Hours

The Administrative Offices of the Tyrrell County Sheriff’s Office normal operating hours will be daily from 7:00 am to 5:00 pm excluding weekends and holidays. You may reach the Sheriff’s Office at 796-2251 during normal operating hours. Please dial 911 for all emergencies. After 5:00 pm and on holidays and weekends if you have an emergency please call 911. If you need to speak with a Deputy after hours, holidays, and on weekends please call 473-3444.
